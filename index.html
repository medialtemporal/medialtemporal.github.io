About me

I'm a third-year student at the University of Western Ontario, completing an Honours Specialization in Neuroscience and working as a News Editor for the student newspaper, the Western Gazette.

I'm interested in research, mental health advocacy, and computer science. My goal is to pursue graduate studies in the area of computational psychiatry or neuroscience and combine these interests.

Previously, I've worked at the youth mental health non-profit Jack.org as a summer intern, where I wrote literature reviews and led focus groups; and the Toronto Rehabilitation Institute, where I conducted an items analysis using R of data from a study on traumatic brain injury.
