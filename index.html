about me

I'm a second-year student at the University of Western Ontario, pursuing a Honours Specialization in Neuroscience. I'm passionate about neuroscience research, mental health advocacy, and creative writing.

Currently, I'm a summer research student at the Toronto Rehabilitation Institute, where I'm evaluating the impact of a spatial navigation intervention on patients with traumatic brain injury and multiple sclerosis.

I've always been fascinated by web design, and this summer I finally decided to jump in and learn more. This website was built using HTML and CSS with hopefully some JavaScript forthcoming :) Thank you for being here and I hope you have a good time!

Feel free to contact me at spersa46@uwo.ca.
